Karimeen Pollichathu

                   
                
               

                The way fish is prepared in Kerala is quite eccentric and one of a kind. 
             Fish is basically a staple, and Karimeen Pollichathu, it's a standout dish.
             This Kerala dish is pretty famous in Alleppey.Karimeen Fry is a famous 
             seafood fry mainly served in the house boats for lunch. This recipe is 
             an exotic dish of Fish fry cooked in Kerala style. The Karimeen fry is 
             a spicy fish fry coated with lively masala. Karimeen is one of the most 
             important fish species in kerala state and is also the state fish 
             of Kerala.
