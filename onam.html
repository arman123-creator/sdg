                Parippu curry is basically a Kerala-style dal curry that is made from 
             small gram and ghee. There is a good balance of spices and chillies in the 
             curry to bring out mouth-watering scents and complement the rice it is 
             usually served with. For all the vegetarians out there who would not be 
             able to delve into the delicious array of non-vegetarian delicacies of Kerala
             cuisine.
